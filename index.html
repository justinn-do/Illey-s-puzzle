A number stands before six gates, numbered from 2 to 7, as 1 is bad luck.

Gate II leaves 1 key,
Gate III drops 2,
Gate IV surrenders 3,
Gate V forfeits 4,
Gate VI yields 5,
But the final gate, the 7th, opens for nothing.

Beyond these gates lies a symbol of three lines, born from the mind of a man from Brunswick.

Speak the name.
